THE DAW BY ARTISTS FOR ARTISTS

FEATURES

LIVE COLLAB

Our live collaboration system allows you and your friends to make music together even if you're in the United Kingdom and your friend is in the United States. As long as you both have internet you're all good.

EASY TO USE

You can be a beginner just getting into music or an experienced musician with years of knowledge and still be able to grasp and learn how to use this DAW with simplicity.

AFFORDABLE

Our DAW is meant to be affordable so that a wide variety of people can make music if they wish. The base version is FREE and has no restrictions or limits on making music. The paid versions come with more features such as access to the theme store, live collaboration, scripting, messaging, name changing and many more features.
